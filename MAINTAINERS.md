oci-delta Maintainers

oci-delta is a community project maintained by volunteers. They may be contacted directly in case of a security vulnerability.

However, if possible, please use GitHub's private security vulnerability reporting feature by pressing the "Report a vulnerability" button on the repository's [security](https://github.com/containers/oci-delta/security) page.
